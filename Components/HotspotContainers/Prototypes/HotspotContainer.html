Description

Devista is born to be a menace. It’s handcrafted with specialised components that allow you to keep the ball at your feet and away from opponents.

It’s engineered from sustainably-sourced, high performance materials to keep your game impacting the pitch, rather than the planet.

The result? A performance boot that changes the game.

Materials

Here at Sokito we’re proud to offer the most eco-friendly football boots in the world, but due to current material limitations and the fact that we never compromise on performance, our boots are not yet 100% sustainable.

As a planet positive brand, we’re committed to transparency. Here are the materials that we currently have to use but are working hard to find alternatives for:

  • Stud tips – Virgin TPU – To guarantee abrasion resistance.

  • Lasting insole – Polypropylene – To avoid stud pressure.

  • Eyestay reinforcement - Fibreglass – To make sure lace holes don’t tear.

  • Foam padding – Virgin materials – For consistent softness and rebound.

  • Adhesive – Solvent based – To keep the boot in one piece.

Non-slip grip

Our eco-suede lining is 85% recycled polyester, sourced from discarded plastic bottles. The material has a suede type texture, for a super soft ‘slipper-like’ touch and a non-slip grippy texture, to lock the foot into place.

Engineered lacing system

Off-centre lacing opens surface area on the medial side of the shoe (the strike zone), allowing for cleaner contact,higher levels of control and greater shot power.

Comfort tow padding

The ‘package’ (combination of materials) in the toe area has been specifically designed to optimise both comfort and performance, with a mix of soft upper materials and strategically placed padding and protection.

Crafted by hand

European production allows for tighter controls on quality and employee working conditions, whilst minimising supply chain emissions. Our handmade production methods allow us to maximise material optimisation, ensuring less waste materials.
